Clipboard Access Required

To paste content from your clipboard, this extension needs clipboard access permissions.

How to grant permission:

  1. Look for the clipboard permission dialog in your browser
  2. Click "Allow" to grant clipboard access
  3. You'll be automatically redirected back to your original page

Once you grant permission, you'll be able to paste images and text directly into file inputs.

Note: This window will close automatically after clipboard access is granted.
